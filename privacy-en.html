Privacy Policy

App: Enger – Agent App
Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Enger app collects, uses, and protects your data.

2. Data Collected

The app may collect and store locally on your device:

  • Client data (name, address, phone, email, fiscal code)
  • Visit and phone call records
  • Appointments and notes
  • Location data (for map and visit features)

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely for app functionality:

  • Managing your client portfolio
  • Logging visits and calls
  • Generating activity reports
  • Backup and sync with Google Drive (optional)

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit data to external servers without your explicit consent. Google Drive backup requires your Google authentication and is optional.

5. Permissions Required

  • Location: for logging client visits
  • Calendar: for managing appointments
  • Storage: for saving reports and backups
  • Camera: for barcode scanning
  • Notifications: for reminders and alerts

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use:

  • Google Maps – for map display
  • Google Drive – for optional backup
  • Gemini AI – for optional AI features

7. Your Rights

You may delete all your data at any time using the reset function in the app settings.
